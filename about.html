About NITK Photography Club

The NITK Photography Club is a community of passionate photographers who capture moments, tell stories, and explore creativity through the lens.

Our Vision

To build a creative space where students can explore photography, learn visual storytelling, and capture the beauty of life around them.

What We Do

We organize photo walks, workshops, competitions, event coverage, and collaborative projects for students interested in photography.

Our Community

The club brings together photographers of all skill levels. Members learn from each other and grow through shared experiences.

Club Activities

📷 Photo Walks
🌅 Landscape & Nature Shoots
🎭 Event Coverage
🖼 Exhibitions
